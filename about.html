About Sabra

Where warmth
meets intention.

Sabra is an interior designer based in the heart of family-centered living. With a background rooted in both the functional and the beautiful, she approaches every project with one question: how does this family actually use this space?

Her work is known for its warmth, its restraint, and its ability to make even the most demanding family home feel collected and calm. Nothing feels overdone. Everything feels intentional.
