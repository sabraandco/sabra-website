What We Offer

Design services built
around your life.

Three packages designed to meet you wherever you are, whether you want the full vision to execute yourself, or you want us to handle every last detail.

One
The Design
Blueprint
Starting from $800 to $1,500 / room

"I want the full vision, I'll take it from there."

We go through the entire design process together, every decision, every detail, so you walk away with a complete plan you can execute on your own timeline and budget.

What's included
  • Initial style consultation (video call)
  • Custom mood board & concept direction
  • 3D room rendering
  • Furniture & decor floor plan
  • Curated shopping list with links & sourcing notes
  • Paint palette with sample recommendations
  • Two rounds of revisions
  • Final design presentation deck

Design fee only. Product costs are separate and purchased directly by you from the provided shopping list.

The Peaceful
Home Edit
$5,000 to $15,000 / room

"I want it done, beautifully, seamlessly, completely."

We handle everything from first sketch to final pillow. Full design, sourcing, ordering, delivery coordination, installation, and final styling, so you walk in and it's already home.

Everything in Blueprint, plus
  • In-home consultations (up to 3)
  • Full procurement & vendor management
  • Trade-pricing access on furniture & decor
  • Delivery coordination & installation day
  • Styling & final accessorizing on-site
  • Punch list & post-install follow-up

Design fee separate from furnishings. We source at trade rates and pass the savings to you. Typical all-in: $15,000 to $50,000+ per room.

Everything in Peaceful Home, plus
